Fuel Density Calculation app hosted on the Apple App Store.

About

Who dislikes math? This app is specifically designed for wide-body aircrews (Boeing & Airbus) to compare fuel calculations (incorporating density), between what was uploaded (i.e., received) and the actual fuel on board (i.e., FOB).
